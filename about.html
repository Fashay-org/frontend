What is fashay?

Fashay combines artificial intelligence with insights from top stylists to democratize personal styling. We believe everyone deserves access to professional fashion guidance—so we've created an AI platform that makes it accessible to university students everywhere.

Why Choose Fashay?

AI Stylists

Our AI stylists emulate the expertise of real professionals, offering a unique mix of personalization and insight.

Tailored Recommendations

Upload your wardrobe images and receive personalized styling suggestions for any occasion.

Style Comparison

Compare styles with peers and discover fresh ideas within your university community.

Sustainable Fashion

Promote sharing and exchanging fashion within your community for an eco-friendly experience.
